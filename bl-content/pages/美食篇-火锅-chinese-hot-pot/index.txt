<p>火锅算是我们中华美食的标志了，厌倦了西餐后，我开始尝试做各种美食，火锅当属简单容易又很美味的首选，由于在国外食材有限，所以一切从简。</p>
<p>Chinese hot pot is very basic and common Chinese food, and very easy to cook , after having English food for long time, I start to miss Chinese food so much, so I  learn to cook hot pot.</p>
<p>首先我在亚马逊上买了一个便宜的火锅（ <a href="https://www.amazon.co.uk/Stainless-steel-f%20%20ondue-mandarin-chafing/dp/B00KKC1EV2/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&amp;qid=1531840260&amp;sr=8-1&amp;keywords=stainless+steel+fondue+pot+mandarin+duck+chafing+dish">Amazon Hotpot Pan</a> ），因为从国内香港寄过来的所以便宜，但很实用。还买了迷你型的煤气，这样想在哪里吃都可以带到哪。接下来就是食材了，在国外食材没有国内那么丰富，我在中超买到有羊肉卷，虾，丸子，土豆，豆腐，豆腐皮，生菜，蘑菇，海带，木耳基本上这些就够了，火锅底料是我从国内带来的，当然在中超也可以买到，附一张火锅图片。</p>
<p>I bought a hotpot pan from amazon, the hotpot food you can buy from Chinese supermarket, so the food I  bought for hotpot is lamb roll, shrimp, potato, tofu, lettuce, mushroom, fish meatball, actually you can buy anything that you like, because hotpot is use water to boil the food and dip sauce to eat.  hotpot sauce and dip sauce ,its easy to find in Chinese supermarket.</p>
<p>招待过几次老公的朋友，他们都赞口不绝呢，有一个朋友回去也买了火锅，跟我们一样自己做的吃，顿时觉得很有成就感，能把我们的中华美食教给外国朋友，觉得很开心。</p>
<p>We treat some friends ,they really like it , a friend also bought a hotpot pan and cook it like I told her, I feel so happy that they like Chinese food .</p>
